About Me

I enrolled in the Intro to Programming class at Epicodus because I wanted to challenge myself and explore programming as a possible career path. I grew up in a suburb outside of Portland called West Linn. I attended Lewis & Clark College in Portland, and majored in International Affairs and Political Economics. I currently live in the Kerns neighborhood with my boyfriend and our two cats.

Since I was young, I've been drawn to the visual arts. The aspect of programming that interests me the most is the intersection of creative problem solving and design, and I'm excited to explore its possibilities and limitations over the course of this class.
